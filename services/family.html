Family Visa Categories

Spouse & Partner Visas

Reunite with your spouse or partner for temporary visits or permanent relocation.

  • Marriage-based visas
  • De facto partner visas
  • Fiancé(e) visas
  • Same-sex partner visas

Parent Visas

Bring your parents to join you for temporary stays or permanent residency.

  • Parent visitor visas
  • Parent sponsorship
  • Aged parent visas
  • Contributory parent visas

Child & Dependent Visas

Reunite with your children or include them in your immigration application.

  • Child visitor visas
  • Dependent child visas
  • Adoption visas
  • Orphan relative visas

Sponsor Eligibility

  • Must be a citizen or permanent resident
  • Meet minimum income requirements
  • Provide adequate accommodation
  • No serious criminal convictions
  • Ability to support family members
  • Sign sponsorship undertaking

Applicant Requirements

  • Valid passport and travel documents
  • Pass medical examination
  • Clear police certificates
  • Genuine relationship evidence
  • Meet language requirements (if applicable)
  • Pay applicable visa fees
